Our services

What we do

One Stop Shop

Convenience is a key attribute most of today’s women look for when planning an event as personal as the Wedding Day. Brides would find that their canvassing time is reduced with Wedding Connections mainly because suppliers from various categories are found under one roof.

The Wedding Connections One-Stop Bridal Shop is a 160-square meter showroom where suppliers and clients can hold private meetings within the Makati Central Business District.

Wedding Coordination

Wedding Connections has an in-house team of highly dedicated and knowledgeable event coordinators who have in-depth experience in corporate and personal celebrations, most especially weddings. The team identifies and aligns the client’s budget and individual personalities to arrive at the best possible event our partnership can produce. The key to a seamless wedding is indeed in the details. Rest assured that the Wedding Connections team has everything covered.

Event Styling

Thematic weddings are common nowadays and it is usually the couple’s choice how to incorporate their personalities into a common motif for the big day. Wedding Connections has an in-house event stylist which ensures that your event will reflect your vision from the moment your guests step into your reception venue. From simple to elaborate, let us transform your ideas to tangible details that will make a cohesive and workable picturesque venue.
