About us

Service is the heart of our business

Wedding Connections is a one-stop bridal and debut shop located along Pasong Tamo in Makati. Our 160 square-meter showroom houses suppliers from various parts of the bridal industry. Everything a couple needs on their big day, we at Wedding Connections have it all in one roof!

Our Mission

To constantly strive to meet and exceed our customer's needs by providing courteous, affordable, quality and exceptional customer service.

When you hire us, we make sure that you are in full control of everything, and we make sure that your dream wedding will be a reality. Together with our partner suppliers, we will ensure that your big day will be flawless and memorable.

Our Vision

To be the top bridal service provider here in the Philippines.

As our slogan says, "Service is the heart of our business", we put our hearts in every event we work on and we make sure that everything works as it should be. With this ethos that we have we will strive to be the go-to wedding coordinator of every Filipino.
